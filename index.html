About Me

I am a creative thinker and playful tinkerer who is driven by unique and challenging problems. A nimble coder, I strive for simple, elegant solutions. My ultimate passion is to fuse art and design with electronics, code, sensors, wires, and solder.

Over the last five years, I have been recruited to produce work for outstanding digital shops around Chicago. In my downtime, I aspire to create magic by joining forces with kick-ass artists, designers and developers throughout the midwest. I am always seeking new challenges and collaborations.

You can find me elsewhere on the web: follow me on Twitter, browse my open-source code on GitHub, and see my professional network on LinkedIn.
